  1. Preheat oven to 400 Farenheit
  2. Put tapioca flour in a large bowl
  3. Pour milk into a large pan along with the vegetable oil and salt
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer on medium/high heat
  5. Once bubbles start forming on the outer edge, remove from the heat and immediately add it to the tapioca flour
  6. Mix until consistency is dough-like
  7. Wait five minutes before adding both cheeses
  8. Add the egg and stir the mixture together
  9. Rub olive oil or flour on hands to prevent sticking
  10. Rub dough into small balls or use an ice cream scooper to portion into a mini muffin pan
  11. Place into oven for approximately 20 minutes or until they are a light yellow color
  12. Serve fresh
